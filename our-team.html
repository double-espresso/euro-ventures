Lucian Wagner

Lucian Wagner

General Partner

Lucian has a truly global outlook with an extensive set of contacts in North America, Europe, and Asia. He is currently a General Partner at EuroUS Ventures LLC, Chairman & Co-founder of Launch in US Alliance, LLC and EuroUS Accelerator, Inc. and his main focus now is helping European companies gain traction in the US. In this function, he lectures frequently at seminars throughout Europe and the US and helps organize study trips and visits to Boston and Silicon Valley. He is a current and past member of the Board of several European ICT companies and advises them on Marketing, Financing and General Strategy issues.

Before joining EuroUS Ventures, he was a CEO and/or President and Co-founder of several enterprise software companies, such as FabCentric, a Yield Management software company, where he raised several million dollars in VC funding and which he grew to more than 25 employees before it was sold to another publicly traded software company.

Previously, Lucian spent more than 20 years in the semiconductor equipment industry, holding a series of positions in Sales and Marketing, based out of Switzerland and then Boston, MA.

Lucian was born in Germany, educated in the French school system, and went on to earn a B.Sc. in International Economics from the School of Foreign Service at Georgetown University and an MBA from INSEAD in 1981.

Vincent Titolo

Vincent Titolo

General Partner

Vincent has over forty years of domestic and international executive experience in both the corporate and investment sectors. He has been a board member and an advisor with two successful startups in the financial services area focused on clean energy. He sat as a member of the Board of Directors of a leading company in the SaaS market. He founded and successfully managed a VC Fund focused on investments in later stage EU technology companies and brought their offerings to the US market. He was the Chairman & CEO of an application software, compression technologies & network solutions Company with offices in the US, Europe & Asia. He was the CEO of a successful private equity LBO fund. He served as a Senior Vice President and CFO of three NYSE companies, two of which were Fortune 500 companies: Ampex (video, audio and computer products), Tymshare (application software for Fortune 1000 & internet technology) & Shaklee (consumer products). He has managed several public offerings on the NASDAQ & NYSE exchanges. He has actively served on corporate boards in the US, France, Germany, England and Japan.

His focus over the years has been on Business & Strategic Planning, Growth Strategies, Partnering Agreements, Market Strategies, Corporate Governance, Information Technology, Corporate restructuring, VC funding, Private Placements, IPO and Mergers and Acquisitions. His credentials include a BS in Economics from Fordham University, and he has served as a Guest Lecturer at Fordham University’s, Stanford University’s, and Santa Clara University’s Graduate Schools of Business.

Matthew Hua

Matthew Hua

Business Analyst

Matthew Hua is the Business Analyst for EuroUS Ventures, who has two decades of healthcare industry experience, including successful start-ups. He has industry insights into the field of medical devices, HIV support & research and diagnostic teleradiology. He is acutely sensitive of what workflow processes are required from the ‘pure theatrical innovative idea’ to the ‘end product’ the customer receives.
