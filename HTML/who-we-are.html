Investing in US Growth

EuroUS Ventures invests in companies which have passed the significant early stage hurdles of product and market acceptance by sheer virtue of already being up and running somewhere in Europe. Our part of a funding round will be used by the company for the express purpose of developing a US operation. We are always co-investing with some of the top investment firms, family offices and private individuals.

The additional ingredients for this success are:
- Network: Tap into the resources of our huge business network in the US, especially Boston /New England and Northern California. We can help with all parts of US market entry and the result are companies as agile, savvy and swift as their homegrown competitors.

- Operational Support: US-based Ventures Partners with strong operational backgrounds running and exiting US start-ups.

- Access to senior management talent to help develop US operations, especially in the area of Marketing and Sales. When it is time to think about an exit, we know the relevant boutique investment banks and power lawyers that can turn a so-so deal into a real payout for the European Founders and investors.
